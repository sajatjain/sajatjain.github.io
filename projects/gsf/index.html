GSF: Placing Founders First

GSF is a platform for founders by founders, that tries to live by a stringent set of values. We seek these values in the entrepreneurs we work with.

  • A Sense of Direction

    Exceptional Founders leave their footprints behind. We wish to back entrepreneurs who have a strong sense of purpose.

  • Build a Team of "Ace" Players

    We believe successful founders are people magnets and attract other "Ace" players.

  • High Integrity & High EQ

    We like founders who act like owners. Such founders are perpetually curious, independent in their thinking, and have not only high IQ but also an ability to consistently seek high-quality output.

  • Innovators, Disruptors and Ideators

    We like founders who change and disrupt things. They constantly move forward. They have new ideas on how to do things better. They invent the future.

  • Build Long Term and Sustainable Value

    We believe that founders who are secure in themselves create long term and sustainable value.

  • Risk Takers, Apolitical, Eclectic and Quirky

    Visionary founders are risk-takers who take tough decisions in an environment of ambiguity. They seek transparency and are self-confident about their idiosyncrasies.

Apply to GSF - FAQs

What stage do we invest in?
We invest in startups at the incubation, pre-seed and seed stage and have the ability to follow-on in your subsequent rounds. Our preference: to be the first cheque in your startup journey.
How much do we invest?
We invest between $100,000 - $500,000 in our first cheque, with the ability to follow-on in your subsequent rounds.
Do we have a sector preference?
We’re focused on tech-led startups and are completely sector agnostic. We’ve invested in category defining businesses ranging from Consumer-Tech to FinTech to SAAS. More recently, we’ve made our first investments in Web 3.0/Crypto, AgriTech and Robotics. You can view our portfolio here.
When is the right time to get in touch?
We’re first cheque investors and connect with founders everyday ranging from those of you at an idea stage to those who have launched with early product-market fit and some customer traction.
What else can I expect from GSF?
As a first-mover in India’s tech ecosystem, raising capital from GSF opens doors to hundreds of mentors from our network - ranging from founders from a diverse range of backgrounds, VCs, CXOs and sector experts. You also get access to over 200 GSF-funded founders who have build their startups from the idea stage onwards to scaling to millions of dollars in revenues.
How should I connect with you?
Fill out the form on this page or email us at team@gsfindia.com. We reply to all messages within the week.
