About GSF Academy 2022

GSF ACADEMY FAQs

What is GSF Academy?
GSF Academy is a 90-day program for entrepreneurs building from India. We bring together leaders (founders, VCs, and CxOs) from across the startup ecosystem to give sessions on company-building fundamentals, past startup experiences, and sector-specific learnings. Along with this, community learning is an integral part of the Academy where we will facilitate weekly interactions and group activities among peer founders. This is not an accelerator program – there is no equity stake, no terms and conditions.
How is the program structured? What is the time commitment?
This is a twelve-week program. Every week we will have two mentoring sessions on weekdays and one community engagement ‘Circle 8’ session on Saturdays. Sessions are 1.5 hours long and take place in the evenings between 5-7pm.
When do I apply? When can I expect a response?
Applications are open till 15 April 2022. We are carefully reviewing applications from founding teams across India. Startups invited to participate will be announced in the last week of April.
What is the program fee?
There is a program fee of INR 15,000 per founder due before the start of the Academy. There is no equity stake and no terms and conditions.
Who is eligible to participate?
The GSF Academy is open for all, no matter where you are in the entrepreneurship journey. A founder with intriguing startup ideas scribbled on paper is just as much of a fit as a company with early PMF and a handful of paying customers.
What’s in it for the applicant/founder?
- Invite-only masterclasses from leading founders, VCs, and CxOs
- Engagement with a peer community of exceptional founders
- Access to the GSF team for 9 weeks and beyond
Will we be engaging with the other entrepreneurs in the Academy?
Absolutely, community interactions and peer learning are the cornerstone of the Academy. We will have weekly sessions where you will be collaborating with other entrepreneurs on various business-building exercises. These sessions will take place in groups of eight called ‘Circle 8’, and founders will tackle various company specific challenges like GTM, market research, pricing, fundraising, hiring, etc.
Why is GSF doing an Academy program?
We feel amazing founders sometimes don’t have access to equally exceptional talent and mentorship. GSF aims to democratize this and create a more supportive environment that enables others to succeed. The Academy also gives us a chance to work with founders early in their entrepreneurial path and potentially invest in the company later.
Will I need to travel?
No, the GSF Academy is an online program.
Will there be a demo day?
Yes, you will get an opportunity to present your startup to GSF and a select group of other investors at the end of the program.

About GSF Fintech Project

GSF Fintech Project is an 8-week program designed to accelerate the growth of fintech startups. We are looking for innovative startups that are solving problems in core banking services, wealth management, digital payments, cross-border payments, blockchain, SME lending, Neo-banks, Crypto and NFT. FAQs

Format?
Ans We will select 10 teams for this project. Unlike an accelerator or bootcamp program, we don’t have daily time-bound sessions to school you. We host talks twice a week and invite topic experts to speak. Typically, these speakers are fintech startup founders, venture capitalists and industry veterans.
Funding?
Ans We will invest $50k as a seed capital in the startup using SAFE notes. These SAFE notes are founder-investor friendly notes that help them to decide on a valuation.
Why should you join GSF FP’21?
Ans This project is more than just money. Our main objective is to engage with you by providing mentorship, right direction and preparation to scale. We provide you with a large network of experts and investors that you can leverage to scale your business and get prepared for the next round of funding.
Who can join GSF FP’21?
Ans We are looking for bright founders that have a conviction to solve complex problems through innovation. We are happy to back your team even if you have just built your MVP or have already attained a product market fit.
Our Philosophy?
Ans We invest in people before ideas. We wish to back entrepreneurs who have a strong sense of purpose. Our key objective is to spur innovation and our larger mission is to provide informed, knowledgeable mentorship capital to the start-ups.

FAQs

What stage do we invest in?
We invest in startups at the incubation, pre-seed and seed stage and have the ability to follow-on in your subsequent rounds. Our preference: to be the first cheque in your startup journey.
How much do we invest?
We invest between $100,000 - $500,000 in our first cheque, with the ability to follow-on in your subsequent rounds.
Do we have a sector preference?
We’re focused on tech-led startups and are completely sector agnostic. We’ve invested in category defining businesses ranging from Consumer-Tech to FinTech to SAAS. More recently, we’ve made our first investments in Web 3.0/Crypto, AgriTech and Robotics. You can view our portfolio here.
When is the right time to get in touch?
We’re first cheque investors and connect with founders everyday ranging from those of you at an idea stage to those who have launched with early product-market fit and some customer traction.
What else can I expect from GSF?
As a first-mover in India’s tech ecosystem, raising capital from GSF opens doors to hundreds of mentors from our network - ranging from founders from a diverse range of backgrounds, VCs, CXOs and sector experts. You also get access to over 200 GSF-funded founders who have build their startups from the idea stage onwards to scaling to millions of dollars in revenues.
