About Us

We are a group of serial entrepreneurs passionate about building technology that touches human lives in some positive sense.

We have built and sold successful product companies in recent years. We noticed that despite there being so many software outsourcing companies and agencies, none of them takes a Product Development approach. Nobody in these agencies has built a real tech product and launched successfully in the market. We are sure a lot of them are pretty good at delivering turnkey projects, but building a digital product needs much more.

LaunchPod Labs was built with an idea of setting up a factory for building cutting edge digital products with the best talent you can find anywhere. Our goal is to bring your ideas to life!
